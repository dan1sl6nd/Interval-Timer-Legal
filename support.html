Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

If you have Premium and reinstalled the app, navigate to Settings > Restore Purchases to regain access to your features.

Why aren't my notifications working?

Ensure that you have granted Interval Timer permission to send notifications in your iOS Settings app under Notifications > Interval Timer.
