# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: February 25, 2026

## Introduction
Welcome to Interval Timer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will explain how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

## The Data We Collect About You
Interval Timer may collect and process the following data:
- **Health Data**: If you opt-in to Apple Health integration, we read and write workout logs to your device's Health app. This data is processed entirely on your device and is not collected or stored on our servers.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymized data on how you interact with the app.

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide and maintain the Interval Timer application.
- To sync specific workout sessions with Apple Health, strictly with your explicit permission.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your identifiable data to outside parties. All Health data integrations remain strictly local to your device and your personal iCloud account.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
